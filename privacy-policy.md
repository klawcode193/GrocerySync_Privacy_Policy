**2. Data We Collect and Why**
GrocerySync is designed to process as little personal data as possible. The categories of data we handle are:
- App content you create: shopping lists, items, categories, recipes, and related content. This data is stored on your
  own device and, if you enable Google Drive sync, in a visible folder named "GrocerySync" in your personal Google Drive
  account. We do not store this data on our own servers. You can inspect or delete the GrocerySync folder and sync files
  directly from Google Drive.
- Google account information (optional): if you sign in with Google to enable sync, we receive your Google account name and email address from Google's OAuth service. This is used solely to identify your sync session and is never stored on our servers.
- Shared list files: If you choose to share a list, GrocerySync creates a separate Google Drive file for that shared
  list and grants link-based access. Anyone with the sharing link may be able to view and edit that shared list file
  until sharing is stopped or the file/permissions are removed.
- Device preferences: theme, notification, and display preferences stored locally on your device via the operating system's standard storage APIs.
- Advertising identifiers (optional): if you have not purchased the ad-free upgrade, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect advertising identifiers and related data to serve contextual advertisements. See Section 7 for details.

We do not operate our own servers or backend infrastructure. We do not collect analytics, crash reports, usage statistics, or behavioral tracking data beyond what Google's SDKs collect on our behalf (described below).

**3. Legal Basis for Processing (EEA/UK Users)**
Under the GDPR and UK GDPR, we rely on the following legal bases:
- Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)): processing necessary to provide the app's core functionality.
- Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): improving app stability and preventing misuse.
- Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): for advertising identifiers used to serve personalised ads, where required by applicable law.

**4. How We Use Your Data**
Your data is used only to operate the app, sync across devices using your Google Drive account if enabled, support optional shared lists, personalise your experience, and display advertisements in the free tier. We do not sell, rent,
  or trade your personal data to any third party.
**5. Data Storage and International Transfers**
Your grocery data is stored locally on your device and, if you enable sync, in your own Google Drive account in a visible folder named "GrocerySync". The primary sync file is stored as "GrocerySync/grocerysync.json". We do not store your grocery data on our own servers.
GrocerySync uses visible Google Drive files so you can inspect, export, or delete your sync data directly from Google Drive. Shared lists are stored as separate Google Drive files and may be accessible to anyone with the sharing link if you choose to share them.

When you use Google Sign-In or Google Drive sync, your data is processed by Google LLC under Google's terms, privacy policy, and approved transfer safeguards.

**6. Data Retention**
Your grocery data is retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. Synced data in Google Drive is retained until you delete the GrocerySync folder, delete the relevant GrocerySync files from your Google Drive account, or revoke the app's access. Shared list files may remain accessible to people with the sharing link until you delete the file or remove its sharing permissions.

For shared lists, anyone with the sharing link may have access according to the Google Drive permissions created when you share the list.
